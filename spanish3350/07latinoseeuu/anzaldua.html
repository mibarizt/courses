Gloria Anzaldúa (1942-2004)

Gloria Anzaldúa was a Chicana tejana lesbian-feminist poet and fiction writer. She taught Chicana studies, feminist studies, and creative writing in various universities and conducted writing workshops around the country. Her work appeared in many venues.
